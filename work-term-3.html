Co-op Work Term 3

Rodan Energy Solutions Inc. Software Developer

Abstract/Introduction

For my third work term, I had the opportunity to work at Rodan Energy Solutions as a Software Developer. I joined the Energy Management Information System (EMIS) team, where I had the unique chance to contribute to a new project called the Bid Automation API. This internal application streamlines access to the Ontario Energy Market, making it easier for market operators at our company to manage and adjust bids effectively.

Information About The EMIS Team and Rodan Energy

Since 2003, Rodan Energy Solutions has expanded from a local metering service provider into a leading energy management company across North America. We offer innovative solutions to large commercial and industrial energy users, as well as power distribution and generation companies.

The EMIS team works on optimizing energy usage for facilities by implementing Energy Management Information Systems (EMIS). These systems help businesses, like hotels and universities, understand their energy consumption and find ways to use energy more efficiently without sacrificing comfort or performance.

An EMIS allows for real-time monitoring of energy usage, helping to identify peak hours and areas of high consumption. With automatic alerts, it makes managing energy easier by notifying users of abnormal events, and it finds opportunities to save energy by analyzing data and providing actionable insights. Overall, the EMIS team helps businesses save money, improve efficiency, and reduce their environmental impact.

I developed over 13 RESTful endpoints using ASP.NET Core, deploying them with Swagger to enhance usability for internal market operators. Additionally, I leveraged Microsoft Azure services to authenticate users and securely store confidential data in Azure Key Vault, improving security for more than 25 market participants.
