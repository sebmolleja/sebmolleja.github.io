Co-op Work Term 4

ATS Corporation. Software Developer

Abstract/Introduction

For my fourth work term, I had the opportunity to work at ATS Corporation, a company that provides automation solutions worldwide. They specialize in planning, designing, building, commissioning, and servicing automated manufacturing and assembly systems, including automation products and test solutions. I joined the Digital Analytics Team in the Innovation department as a Software Developer.

Information About The Innovation Team and ATS Corporation

I worked under ATS Life Sciences, a division of ATS Corporation that specializes in automated manufacturing and assembly solutions for the medical, pharmaceutical, and diagnostic industries. They provide end-to-end automation to help MedTech and pharma manufacturers bring life-changing products to market faster.

They also integrate AI, cloud computing, and digital twins to optimize manufacturing efficiency and compliance, being the area where I contributed the most.

I was involved in developing a full-stack web and desktop application designed to streamline the design and configuration of automation tooling. It provided both internal teams and customers with an intuitive platform to customize automation solutions efficiently, enhancing productivity and precision in manufacturing processes.
