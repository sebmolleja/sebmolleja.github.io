Co-op Work Term 2

University of Guelph, Software Developer Intern - Greenbug Energy

Abstract/Introduction

For the second half of my first work term I earned the opportunity to work in the Engineering Department and the University of Guelph as a Software Developer Intern. The team I had joined was working on a Archimedes Screw Project in collaboration with a company called Greenbug Energy to produce a web application for users to scale their Screw plans within software implementation. This tab will go over my experience over the 4 months I was there.

Information About The Team and Greenbug Energy

Greenbug Energy specializes in creating, producing, installing, managing, and servicing Archimedes screw generators designed for low head micro hydro sites and other industrial energy recovery applications ranging from 1 to 500kW in capacity. They currently work in collaboration with a research team in the Engineering Department at the University of Guelph to develop and implement software solutions that offer their work to customers via an interactive web application.

Job Description

I contributed to the latest version of Greenbug's primary application, Water Watts, which focused on simulating the creation, planning, and blueprinting of Archimedes Screw Pumps for construction. This comprehensive application incorporated building materials, specific parts, cost considerations, location details, and simulation of the projected pump. In the initial quarter of my tenure, our team was assigned the task of refactoring the older application and integrating it with another foundational system, consolidating both into the main app. Subsequently, my week-to-week responsibilities spanned the entire development spectrum, from front-end to back-end tasks. This included designing home pages, optimizing query time efficiencies, implementing asynchronous worker tasks, and more. The primary programming language utilized was Django, with additional application of Git, Docker, Unix, and Javascript throughout the term.

Working with a talented team of bright individuals was a privilege, as their mentorship and guidance enriched my learning experience. I am more than grateful to have been a part of this team throughout these four months and I will take all the experience I gained with me forward and improve from it.
